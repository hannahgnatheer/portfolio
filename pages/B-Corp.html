B-Corp Analysis

Summary

The data indicated a consistent global increase of B-Corporation certificates from 2007 to 2023, with significant growth observed in Europe, Africa, and certain regions of Asia post-2016. In terms of sector, manufacturing and high-environmental-footprint services had the most rapid growth, signifying a transition towards sustainability in historically resource-intensive sectors. Impact assessment correlations indicated that Community and Customer dimensions are the most significant contributors to elevated total scores, whereas Environment, Workers, and Governance displayed more diminished contribution, implying differences in corporate prioritization of impact areas.



Model Evaluation

Auto-ARIMA accurately predicted sectoral and regional development trends, yielding the most precise projections for low-footprint service industries and rising regions like Africa and South America, whereas European and Oceanic predictions exhibited higher error margins due to increased unpredictability. The ANN identified intricate, non-linear correlations between impact locations and overall scores, however residual analysis revealed diminished accuracy at higher score ranges.
